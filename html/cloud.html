Analysis: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Without delineating between northern and southern hemisphere, there is no correlation between latitude and cloud coverage. In cities located in the Northern hemisphere, there is no significant correlation but a slight negative trend in which the closer to the northern pole there may be slightly less cloud coverage. In cities located in the Southern hemisphere, there is no significant correlation between the percent cloud coverage and distance from the equator. To reiterate, statistically there is no correlation between latitude and cloudiness.
